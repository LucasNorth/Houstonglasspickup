Frequently Asked Questions

When will my pickup occur?
Pickups will take place during the weekends (saturday or sunday). We will coordinate the date of your first pickup with you after receiving your sign up. We will also send a reminder by text and email on the day before a scheduled pickup.
What areas of Houston do you cover?
We serve residents all over Houston. If there's any issue with coverage in your area, we'll let you know.
Where do I get a recycling bin for my glass?
We will provide you with one at no extra charge. This will save you the hassle and inconvenience of finding one that is compatible with our allowable amount of glass for collection.
Can I skip a pickup?
Yes, you can skip a pickup whenever needed (e.g., out of town, not much glass). There is no charge as long as you notify us by the day before the scheduled pickup.
Where do I place the bin for pick up?
The same way as the weekly thrash pick up, or in front of your driveway.
How much glass can be pick up by service ?
Our bins hold 30 Liters (or 7 gallons) of glass. If you have much more than that after your first pickup, we may contact you to discuss options.
